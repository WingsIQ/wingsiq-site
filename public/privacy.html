Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Cobalt Oasis LLC operates WingsIQ at wingsiq.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, password
  • Training data: quiz responses, ATC session data, progress scores
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details
  • Usage data: features used, session duration, pages visited
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Personalize your training experience
  • Process payments and send transactional emails
  • Send product updates (you can opt out any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI and Voice Data

Voice input is processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI Whisper, Anthropic Claude) in real time. Voice recordings are not permanently stored beyond your active session unless you explicitly save it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers (Stripe, Vercel, Anthropic, Resend), when required by law, or in connection with a business transfer.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@wingsiq.ai.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security including HTTPS encryption and access controls. No system is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

9. Children's Privacy

WingsIQ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions: hello@wingsiq.ai
